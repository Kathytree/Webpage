What Dress Style Would You Like?

Height: Stand straight against a wall or doorframe. Measure from the top of your head to your feet.

Bust/Chest: Wrap the measuring tape around the fullest part of your chest, ensuring itâ€™s parallel to the ground.

Waist: Find your natural waistline by bending to one side; this creates a crease. Measure around this point without pulling the tape too tight.

Hips: Measure around the widest part of your hips and ensure the tape remains horizontal.

Shoulder Width: Measure straight across from the tip of one shoulder to the tip of the other.

Arm Length: Start from the point where your shoulder meets your arm. Measure down to your wrist bone, following the curve of your arm.

Inseam: Measure from the top of your inner thigh down to your ankle bone.

Circumference (e.g., neck, arms, thighs): Wrap the tape around these areas snugly, but not tightly.
